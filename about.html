H_art the band is My Favorite since they have a somewhat gospel record off the album dubbed ‘El Shaddai’ that features the super talented record producer Cedo that you can’t seem to get enough of once you listen to it for the very first time. I for one, have had that song on rotation since Friday to this day, I guess that’s what happens when talented artistes and record producers decide to work together.
